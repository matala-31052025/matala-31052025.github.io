Traveling to and staying in Matala

  • How do I get to Crete?

    The easiest way is to fly to Heraklion Airport Nikos Kazantzakis. If you’re in Athens or the Cycladic islands, you can also catch a ferry to Heraklion.

  • How do I get to Matala?

    There are a few options from Heraklion city/(air)port:
    By private transfer: We will organize a private transfer from Heraklion on 31/05 and back on 01/06. If you are interested, please indicate so in our form asap to help us plan and visit this page again later for info like departure times & locations :).
    By car: The road from Heraklion to Matala is an easy drive and plenty of parking is available in and around Matala. You can rent a car at the airport. We are working on negotiating better bulk rental rates for our guests; if you are interested, please indicate so in our form asap and visit this page again later for next steps :).
    By line bus: There are line buses (timetables for 2025 to be announced, stay tuned!) leaving from the bus station in Heraklion.

  • Where do I stay? How do I get around Matala?

    We recommend booking accommodation within walking distance of the village/beach. There are plenty of hotels, rooms and guesthouses (even camping options) within (a 10' walk from) the village. We will provide a list of recommendations on this page soon.
    We aim to negotiate bulk room rate offers for our guests; if you would like a room at a better rate, please indicate so in our form asap and come back to this page again later for next steps :).
