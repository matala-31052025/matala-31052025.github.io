Traveling to and staying in Matala

  • How do I get to Crete?

    The easiest way is to fly to Heraklion Airport Nikos Kazantzakis. If you’re in Athens or the Cycladic islands, you can also catch a ferry to Heraklion.

  • How do I get to Matala?

    There are a few options from Heraklion city/(air)port:
    By private transfer: We will organize a private transfer from Heraklion on 31/05 and back on 01/06. If you are interested, please indicate so in our form as soon as possible to help us plan and visit this page again later for further info :).
    By car: The road from Heraklion to Matala is an easy 1h-1.5h drive, and there’s plenty of parking available in and around Matala. You can rent a car at the airport. If you let us know as soon as possible, we can help secure better rates for you, and we’ll share the details here soon. Please note, however, that you will need to complete the booking process on your own afterwards.
    By line bus: There are multiple daily line buses (timetables for 2025 to be announced, stay tuned!) leaving from the bus station in Heraklion.

  • Where should I stay?

    We recommend booking accommodation within walking distance of the village and beach. There are plenty of options, including hotels, guesthouses, and even camping, all within a 10-minute walk from the village. We’ll soon provide a list of recommendations on this page.

  • Who will arrange my stay?

    Guests are responsible for booking their own accommodation. However, we will negotiate better bulk rates with selected accommodations. If you're interested, please let us know through our form as soon as possible. Once confirmed, you’ll need to handle the booking process directly with the accommodation provider.
